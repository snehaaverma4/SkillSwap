Process

How SkillSwap works

Four simple steps from signing up to your first skill exchange — no payments, no gatekeeping.

01
Register & declare skills
Create your profile. List skills you can teach — be honest, you'll be tested on them.
02
Pass the skill test
Take a short 15-min AI-evaluated test per skill. Pass = verified badge on your profile.
03
Find a match
Search for what you want to learn. If your offer matches their wish list — it's a swap.
04
Teach or earn points
Swap directly with a match, or teach anyone and earn points to spend on your own learning.
Trust & Safety

Only verified teachers

We built a 4-layer system to ensure no ghost profiles, no bluffers, and no wasted time.

📋
Skill declaration at signup
Choose your teaching skills on day one — you can't add new ones without re-testing.
✅
AI-graded skill test
Each skill requires a 15-min test. Score under 70%? You can retake after 48 hours.
⭐
Post-session ratings
Learners rate each session. A rating below 3.5/5 triggers an automatic re-verification.
⏸️
Inactive teacher flag
No teaching in 60 days? Your profile auto-pauses so learners aren't misled.
